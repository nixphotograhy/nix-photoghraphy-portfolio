# Design System: Nix Portfolio (Stitch ID: 5242748537374545145)

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**"The Cinematic Curator" (Docutorial Aesthetic)**
The atmosphere is moody, cinematic, and immersive, evoking the feeling of a high-fashion lookbook or a 35mm film reel. It focuses on raw timing, editorial curation, and intentional asymmetry to create a premium, non-generic experience.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Background (Obsidian) (#131313)**: The primary atmosphere; provides a darkroom backdrop that makes photography pop.
- **Primary (Muted Forest Green) (#bccac2 / #192420)**: Used for functional headers, container tonal shifts, and subtle visual cues.
- **Tertiary (Warm Cream/Sand) (#cac6bc)**: The "soul" of the brand; adds analog warmth and reduces the digital harshness of the dark mode.
- **Base Surface (#0e0e0e)**: The deepest tonal layer, used for inset or well backgrounds.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Headings & Marquee**: `Newsreader` (High-contrast, authoritative serif). Placed asymmetrically, often overlapping image boundaries.
- **UI & Functional Body**: `Manrope` (Geometric, minimalist sans-serif). Used for metadata, labels, and small descriptions.
- **Letter Spacing**: Generous (0.05em) for titles to maintain premium status.

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons**: Sharp squared-off edges (`0px` radius). No borders. Focus on tonal shifts from `surface` to `primary-container`.
- **Cards/Containers**: Flush-to-edge images with sharp corners. Information is often bottom-aligned and asymmetrically balanced.
- **The "No-Line" Rule**: No 1px solid borders. Separation is achieved purely through background color shifts (tonal layering).

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Asymmetry**: Intentional imbalance; e.g., images bleeding off-screen or captions placed in large negative space areas.
- **Whitespace**: Grand gaps between major editorial sections (e.g., `8.5rem` / `spacing-24`).
- **Nesting**: Physical layers using the surface hierarchy (Base -> Mid-Ground -> Elevated).
